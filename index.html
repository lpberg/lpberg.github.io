About Me

I'm an HCI/UX design researcher at Sandia National Laboratories. My interests span a variety of HCI topics including Human-Computer Interaction, User-Centered Design, Human Factors, and Virtual Reality.

My graduate research explored the integration of immersive virtual environments and decision making in the context of product design.
